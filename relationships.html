cloudify.relationships.contained_in¶

A node may only have one outgoing contained_in relationship. This defines the tree heirarchy of the nodes.

Derived from: cloudify.relationships.depends_on

cloudify.relationships.depends_on¶

Properties:

connection_type

default: all_to_all

Describes how connections are handled when node templates on both sides of the relationship are scaled to multiple instances. Valid values are:

all_to_all
all_to_one
